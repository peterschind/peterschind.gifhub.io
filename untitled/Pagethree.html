Flatbush Zombies is a rap group based out of Brooklyn, New York. group is made up of three life long freinds. they adress themselves under the alias of Meechy Darko, Eric the Architect, and Zombie Juice. Eric is the mastermind behind the beats, producing them and rapping on them. He is the most mellow rapper of the group. Meechy Darko, like his name, is a very dark rapper. He has a thick deep voice which he uses to express his strugles of growing up in the streets of New York. Zombie Juice lastly, has a light voice and a very choppy flow when he rhymes. These three men have only a few albums, but every song is well produced and fun to listen to.
